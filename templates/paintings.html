Artist Statement

We all live under surveillance. We pry into each other’s private lives. Technology has become stalkers’ best friend. Everyone stalks and is stalked by others.

I made the portraits look tranquil in order to demonstrate that people have gotten used to this form of surveillance. The stalking tools in the portraits are some of the most commonly used items: a camera, Skype, Facebook and an iPhone.
